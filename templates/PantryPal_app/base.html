FAQ

How do I separate ingredients?

Separate your search ingredients with spaces! Its that easy.

How do I include two word ingredients? (Ex: peanut butter)

Put and underscore between the words of your ingredient.

peanut_butter OR chocolate_ice_cream.

What is the "maximum preparation time"?

This sets the upper limit for the amount of time you wish to devote to cooking your dish. Therefore, if you type 60, some results that may only take 10 minutes can appear. The time is in minutes.

What if I don't type a maximum preparation time?

There is none! Your results will not be capped by any time restrictions.

How are the results organized?

Your results are organized in order of increasing additional ingredients. That means the easyiest recipes for you are first!
